## has toggle mute control

Capable of handling ON/OFF mute control.


### Signature

`<has_toggle_mute_control></has_toggle_mute_control>`


| Parameter | Description |
| --- | --- |
| bool | True/False |


### Example

```xml
<capabilities>
   <has_toggle_mute_control>true</has_toggle_mute_control>
</capabilities>
```
